First Time User Training

The Hotel Effectiveness 200 level provides essential, role-specific skills for first-time users. This training is conveniently located within the My Training section of the Hotel Effectiveness application, ensuring you receive instruction focused on the practical skills relevant to your role.

To access your My Training lessons, please follow these steps:

  1. Log into Hotel Effectiveness.
  2. Click your username in the upper-right corner of the screen.
  3. Select My Training from the dropdown menu.
  4. Click Complete Now to start each lesson in your learning path.
